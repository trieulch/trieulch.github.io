Sales & Products Dashboard

Tools: SQL, Power BI

Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance and product-level insights. It was designed to help business stakeholders monitor revenue trends, identify high-performing products, and understand customer geographical distribution.

Dashboard Features

    Total sales performance tracking
    Customer geographical distribution
    Product category performance
    Revenue trend analysis
    Interactive filtering for deep insights

Key Insights

Total sales reached $24.9M from 25,164 orders.
North America generated the highest sales, contributing $15M.
The Bikes category drove $23.6M in sales with 13,929 units sold.
Revenue tends to increase toward the end of each year, with an average growth rate of 59%.
